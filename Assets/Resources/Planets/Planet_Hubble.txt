Hubble
Hubble, named after famous human astronomer Edwin Hubble, is a Sub-Earth planet in the goldilock zone of the star Ankol-1 means its hosts life. Hubble has an immensely large natural supply of fuel. Because of this fuel is very cheap on this planet making it a popular space ship stop. It is the only planet in the system that has an ocean on it. The planet is low on iron, because they have been building infrastructure and depends on traders and Mining companies. The planet hosts a large colony of humans and there are many native animal species. The animals depend on the special plants that grow on the planet. One species shows a particularly high form of sentience, they are called the Kogras.
